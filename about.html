The Game of Cricket

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players on a field. It is one of the most popular sports in the world, especially in countries like India, Australia, England, and Pakistan.

History of Cricket

Cricket dates back to the 16th century in England and has evolved over the years into different formats including Test, One Day, and T20 cricket.
